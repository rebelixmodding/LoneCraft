----------------Mod Overview

Lone Craft is a mod that actually may make you feel no more alone when you are playing singleplayer. It's a real gift to the player that can't play with his friends because he has no internet or simply because he don't have any. No longer, with Lone Craft, you will feel so much useless in an infinite world. This mod will totally realize your dreams. Ah.. would'n it be wonderfull to have a server where all of your players work at thier occupations peacefully while you are watching them from above your admin-cloud ? Or prehaps you would prefer a world where war is always on and where factions of warriors raise from the ground to reign, then finally fall and leave it's place to the nature to take it's real place ?

These days where you are part of a server are back. It's now your turn to take your sword and unite these lands by the force of the armies you will forge. Your turn to Build up cities with your new workforce, and look at these peasants to cultivate thier farms, to chop down forests, to chase them monsters and to live thier lives. Shall you may be a rich noble, a knight, a lord or a citizen, you now have the choice. Choice to be an heretic that lives in the jungle with his neighbors nearby, choice to be the king and that will rule the world, choice to just live and let live by settling your colony where ever the hell you want. Choice to build yourself a little cozy lakeside manor and invite important people so you can throw ball parties. Choice to go hunt down these scruby creepers with your personnal assistants or your friends. Choice to make yourself some relationships and have people to know you. Chance to hire guards to keep specific places of your castle and to pay bodyguards that will get better stuff and will follow you. Chance to manage the whole domain and to set your servant's very own bed and home. May he sleep on the carpet or may he get a nice bedroom for himself. Because now ..... you are no more ALONE !
